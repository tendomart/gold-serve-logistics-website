OCCUPIER SERVICES Your Resettlement Partner

Setting up office spaces or moving from one workspace to another can get really intense and stressful at some point. When one finds it hard to find a space of preference that falls in one’s budget, it not only delays a company’s operations but also miss out on a big contract just because you don’t have an office space yet. (Most companies consider office space as a primary factor to do business with them) With an established relationship with commercial real estate owners and building managers across the region, we make this process much quicker and hassle-free meeting the set targets for operations.

On top of office spaces, some companies deploy Expats to work on local projects that don’t meet the local human resource expertise. In most cases, there is limited time for the company to orient them and safely induct them in the country due to the urgency of their expertise on these projects which are the core purpose of their arrival in the country. Going on to do house search for the expats lengthens the orientation process of that Expat who is expected to start work in a week’s time or sometimes immediately! By involving us in managing your Expats helps them to do what they came to do as soon as possible as we manage their accommodation even before they land into the country through our corporate relations management.
